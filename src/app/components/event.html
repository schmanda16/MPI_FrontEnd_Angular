DFW Event Planners Networking Session

Networking

Join the DFW chapter of Meeting Professionals International as event planners from all around the metroplex gather to expand their networks! This is a perfect opportunity to meet individuals with careers and goals that look like yours. Light bites and drinks will be served.

This event will be in the Trinity Ballroom at the Omni Dallas Hotel. Tickets purchased before June 1st will have the early-bird price of just $15. After that, ticket price will increase to $25. Valet parking is available and can be validated at the registration booth at the event.
