The following descriptions outline eligibility for membership.

As a member of MPI you agree to follow the MPI Policy Manual standards of membership. In summary;

  • Display high ethical standards; (adhere to MPI Principles of Professionalism);
  • Support MPI global programs and activities designed to assist members or contribute to the benefit of the industry;
  • Share the knowledge one has with others to continue to strengthen the effectiveness of the worldwide industry and its professionals; and
  • Use the services and programs offered by the Association without the expectations of the Association substituting for company marketing and sales activities.
